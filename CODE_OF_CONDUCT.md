# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, trolling, insults, or other conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive behavior may be reported to the project maintainer (Nafis Islam Kabbo). All complaints will be reviewed and investigated.
